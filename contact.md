### Join the Lab

If you are looking for a **graduate or postgraduate position**, please reach out — we are always interested in hearing from motivated scientists.

- **Graduate researchers** interested in computational modeling of microbiomes (ocean, soil, or human gut). This position requires admission into UConn Health's [Biomedical Sciences PhD program](https://health.uconn.edu/graduate-school/academics/programs/ph-d-biomedical-science/), in the Systems Biology Area of Concentration.
- **Postdoctoral researchers** to build integrative multiscale models of cellular systems and expand the Vivarium ecosystem. Projects include whole-cell modeling (primarily *E. coli*), community structure in microbiomes, the design of synthetic cells, or the origins of life. PhD in Computational Biology, Bioengineering, Complex Systems, Physics, Computer Science, or a related field, with Python experience. [Download full postdoc description (PDF)](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/eagmon/eagmon.github.io/master/files/postdoc_SMS.pdf).

Email Eran Agmon at [agmon@uchc.edu](mailto:agmon@uchc.edu) with your CV if you are interested.

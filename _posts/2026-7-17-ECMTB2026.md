title: Eran presents at ECMTB 2026
---

Eran attended the [14th European Conference on Mathematical & Theoretical Biology (ECMTB 2026)](https://ecmtb2026.org), held July 13–17, a joint event organized by the Society for Mathematical Biology (SMB) and the European Society for Mathematical and Theoretical Biology (ESMTB).

He presented on Vivarium at the OpenVT minisymposium, sharing recent work on compositional and multiscale simulation frameworks.

The conference was a wonderful opportunity to connect in person, and Eran had delightful discussions with many scientists he was pleased to see face to face.
